How to use the journal

  1. Start small. Commit to two weeks. A few minutes morning and night is enough.
  2. Notice your body. Ask: Is there any part of my body in pain? and take small action if you can.
  3. Set 3 priorities. Each morning write 3–4 things you want to get done in the next eight hours.
  4. Reflect kindly. At night, notice what made you mad, why, and what you can do tomorrow to make things a little better.
  5. Look for patterns. After a week, re‑read your answers and notice small changes. Progress is often quiet.

This practice is less about perfection and more about presence. Keep your answers short — write the first things that come to mind.
