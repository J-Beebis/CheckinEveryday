Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2025

This website is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or process any personal data. All interactions that involve personal information (such as booking a coaching session or sending a message) take place through trusted third-party services such as Calendly or other secure platforms you choose to use.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • No cookies or analytics tools are used.
  • No contact forms or submissions are stored on this site.
  • No tracking pixels or marketing scripts are implemented.

External Links

Links to scheduling, downloads, or other services open in new tabs and are covered by the privacy policies of those respective providers. Please review their policies for details on how they handle data.

Compliance

This site is built to comply with GDPR (EU), CCPA (California), and IEEE ethical data practices by following a strict policy of data minimization — specifically, no data collection at all. Users maintain full control of their information at all times.

Contact

If you have any privacy questions, please contact Jesur directly through external platforms such as Calendly or social channels listed on Linktree.
