Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2025

This site is intentionally minimal. It does not collect, store, or process any personal data. We redirect visitors to trusted third‑party services for bookings or purchases — for example Calendly or store platforms — and those services govern any data they collect.

What we do

  • No analytics, no tracking pixels, no cookies.
  • No contact forms or data stored on this server.
  • All scheduling and payments occur on external sites — we link to them directly.

GDPR & CCPA & IEEE

By design, this site follows principles of data minimization and user autonomy. Because we do not collect personal data, you maintain full control. For any actions that occur off‑site (bookings, purchases), please consult the privacy policy of the platform you use.

Contact

If you have privacy concerns, reach out via the external links found on the site: Linktree or book a call on Calendly.
